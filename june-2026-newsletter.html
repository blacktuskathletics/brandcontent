Whole-Person Wellness: Recover Like You Train

Here is something we say a lot: healthy people are happy people. And real health is bigger than the hour you spend with us. It is movement, yes, but it is also nutrition, recovery, sleep, and understanding how your body actually works.

This month, give recovery the same respect you give your training. The work you put in only becomes strength while you rest. A few simple places to start:

Sleep is the foundation.

Aim for a consistent bedtime. Seven to nine hours is where your body repairs and adapts.

Eat enough, and eat real food.

Protein at every meal and plenty of water do more for your results than any supplement.

Move on your off days.

A walk, a stretch, some easy mobility. Active recovery beats sitting still.

Pick one of these and give it a real try this month. Small, consistent habits are what move the needle, in here and out there. [Optional: swap in a tip from a coach or a member favorite.]
