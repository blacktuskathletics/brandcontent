Your July 2026 community update: what just happened, and what's coming up.

Subject line: Summer's here, and you all showed up for it
Preview text: Brew Crew laughs, two ultra finishers, a BBQ to save the date for, and a heartfelt note about Coach Eve.

Hey team,

There's a stretch of summer in Edmonton where the days feel like they go on forever, and that's exactly the kind of month we just had. Long evenings, full classes, and a community that keeps showing up for each other. Here's what's been happening, and what's coming.

Last month at Black Tusk

A month built around the people.

Jun 14Brew Crew did it again

The Brew Crew got together on June 14, and as usual the gym crew proved we're just as good at hanging out as we are at training. Good people, good drinks, plenty of laughs. If you've ever wondered whether this place is more than a gym, nights like that are your answer. Watch for the next one.

Two of ours took on the North Sun Ultra

Huge congratulations to Sarah Clark and Tamara Smith, who both crossed the finish line at the North Sun Ultra 30k. Thirty kilometers is no small thing. It's the kind of effort that gets built one ordinary training day at a time, the days nobody sees. We see it, and we're proud of you both. If their story lights a little fire in you, come tell us about it. Every first big goal starts with a conversation.

School's out

To all the kids: congratulations on making it through another year. And to the parents who packed the lunches, survived the early mornings, and held it all together, you made it too. You've earned a summer. Let's make movement part of it, for you and the kids both.

A heartfelt note about Coach Eve

Thank you, Eve.

This one comes with a lot of love. Coach Eve is stepping back from her coaching role with us. Anyone who's trained with Eve knows what she brought to the floor: the care, the attention, the way she made people feel like they belonged here from day one. That kind of coaching leaves a mark, and hers is all over this community.

We're going to miss seeing her in her usual spot, and we're grateful for every rep, every cue, and every encouraging word she gave this gym. Please join us in thanking Eve for everything. Stay tuned for a proper send-off.

Coming up: the next few weeks

A few things to put on your radar this month.

Now liveSummer schedule is in effect

A quick heads-up that our summer schedule change is now live. Take a minute to check the updated class times before you head in, so nobody shows up to a locked door or an empty room. If anything's unclear, message us and we'll sort you out.

On the floorNew equipment has landed

We've added some new equipment to the floor. Come get your hands on it, and if you're not sure how a piece works or how to fit it into your training, grab a coach. That's what we're here for.

AugustSave the date: Black Tusk BBQ

Mark your calendar. We're throwing a Black Tusk BBQ in August, and you're invited. Food, family, and the people who make this community what it is. Details to come, but for now, just save the date so you don't miss it.

Make the most of your summer

Summer is a great time to recommit, to bring a friend, or to finally book that No Sweat Intro you've been thinking about. Book free at blacktuskathletics.com, or just reply and tell us what you're working toward. We read every one.

See you in class,
Coach Jay and the Black Tusk team
